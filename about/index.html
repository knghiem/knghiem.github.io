About

I’m a technologist, programmer, and writer based in Hanoi, Vietnam.

Currently, I’m co-writing a novel and preparing for graduate school applications in Information Studies. I want to work in digital humanities, helping people access, navigate, and study cultural heritage from reliable sources.

I moved to the US at 17 to attend Connecticut College (go Camels!) and was a visiting student at Columbia University for a semester. I lived in Austin, Texas for 3 years while working at Google Cloud in various positions. In 2022, I came back to my hometown to spend time with family and reconnect with my roots, while working at FPT Software to build AI coding assistants.
